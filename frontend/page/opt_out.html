Opt Out of Financial Tracking

You can choose to opt out of financial tracking at any time. When you opt out:

  • We will stop collecting and analyzing your financial data
  • Your existing financial data will no longer be displayed
  • All spending values will be set to zero
  • Your account will remain active for future use

You can opt back in at any time by visiting the settings menu.
